Local Government Center - Data Portal

The Montana State University Local Government Center has created this web site for citizens and government officials to access and visualize information about Montana city and county governments.

Information about Montana cities and counties can be viewed in various interactive formats:

City Data

  • Data tables
  • Comparisons across cities
  • Trend charts
  • Data maps
  • Local Government Review

County Data

  • Data tables
  • Comparisons across counties
  • Trend charts
  • Data maps
  • Local Government Review
